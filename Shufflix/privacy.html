Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 3, 2025

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Sign in with Apple: your Apple ID email and full name (if you choose to share it).
  • App Usage: your liked, skipped, and rated titles to personalize your deck.
  • Diagnostics: anonymous crash/performance data to improve reliability.

We do not sell your data or show ads.

2. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate your account and sync data via Supabase.
  • Remember preferences (likes, skips, ratings).
  • Improve app performance and quality.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Account data is stored securely with Supabase. If you delete your account, we delete associated personal data within a reasonable period, subject to legal requirements.

4. Sharing

We do not share personal information except with service providers that help run the App (e.g., Apple Sign-In, Supabase) or when required by law.

5. Your Choices & Rights

  • Request account/data deletion.
  • Contact us with privacy questions: privacy@shufflix.app.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated in the App.
